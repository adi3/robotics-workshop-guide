# Keep in touch

See how far you can take the tenets of cloud robotics towards benefiting your organization.

---

> Play around with the ROS application and integrate more AWS services into it. There are several ready-to-use ROS [cloud extensions](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/robomaker/latest/dg/cloud-services-integration.html) provided by AWS for you to experiment with, and you can build custom ones yourself using the [AWS SDK](https://aws.amazon.com/tools/).
